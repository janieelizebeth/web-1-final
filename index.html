About Me

Hello! My name is Janie and for my entire life I have been enjoying all things visually beautiful and magnificent. As a 20 year old I decided to challenge myself and pursue a Visual Communications Design degree from Eastern Washington University. I have experience in freelance work and would be nowhere if it weren’t for the support from my family and husband.
